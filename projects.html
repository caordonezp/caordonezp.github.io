Honesty and Justice

Collaborators: William Jiménez-Leal, Daniel Felipe Faguer

Description: How the perception of being treated fairly or unfairly relates to honest or dishonest behavior. Using behavioral experiments, we investigate how experiences of justice or injustice influence honesty and corruption in both individual and institutional contexts.

Psychological Pluralism of Justice Scale

Collaborators: William Jiménez-Leal

Our best theories of distributive justice suggest that several criteria can be used to assess whether a distribution is fair. Using psychometric techniques, we developed and validated a scale to measure how pluralistic justice criteria operate in evaluations of justice, depending on the type of value being assessed
